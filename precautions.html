Precautions for Paddy Leaf Diseases

Here are some precautions to prevent and manage paddy leaf diseases:

  1. Choose disease-resistant rice varieties for cultivation.
  2. Practice crop rotation to break the disease cycle.
  3. Ensure proper field sanitation by removing and destroying crop residues.
  4. Treat seeds with recommended fungicides or bactericides before planting.
  5. Implement proper water management to avoid waterlogged conditions.
  6. Maintain balanced fertilization to keep plants healthy and resistant.
  7. Regularly scout the fields for early detection of disease symptoms.
  8. Use integrated pest management (IPM) strategies for holistic disease control.
